Everything you need to know about Weenify and how it works

Weenify is a Chrome extension for dropshippers that helps you find winning products through real-time market analysis, conduct product research using our extensive database, import items to Shopify with one click, and track competitors' sales with our Product Spy tool.

Installing the Weenify Chrome extension is easy. Simply visit the Chrome Web Store, search for "Weenify," and click "Add to Chrome." Once installed, you'll see the Weenify icon in your browser's toolbar. Click on it to get started with your product research and dropshipping journey.
